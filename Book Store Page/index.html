The "Wings of Fire" is one of such autobiography by visionary scientist Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, who from vert humble beginnings rose to be the President of India. The 3 mistakes of my life

by Chetan Bhagat

The 3 mistakes of my life is the third novel written by eminent Indian Author Chetan Bhagat. Based on cricket, business and religion, the novel is set against the backdrop of beautiful city Ahmedabad. Revolving around three young Indian boys Omi, Ishaan and Govind, the book goes on to narrate how thr three are trying their best to make ends meet in the city. Based on real events, the book starts with a dramatic twist, where Bhagat is reading on e-mail sent by some youung person Govind.
